KEY ARGUMENTS:
--------------
DOME'S POSITION:
- Cepheids and SBF standard candles measure distances (correct observation)
- These distances are consistent with a distance-redshift relationship (correct)
- Therefore the dome model is confirmed (non-sequitur)

OUR REBUTTAL:
1. Scale incompatibility: Dome firmament at 9,086 km; Cepheid galaxies at 10–100 Mpc (10⁷–10⁸× farther)
2. No dome mechanism: The model specifies no aetheric field, no luminosity-distance formula, no redshift equation
3. No geometric derivation: Dome parameters (disc_radius=20,015 km, firmament_height=9,086 km) appear nowhere in any distance-redshift prediction
4. Post-hoc adoption: These distance methods were established 1912–2022; dome added WIN-055 in April 2026
5. Multiple independent methods converge: Cepheids, SBF, TRGB, Type Ia supernovae all agree within ±5% across 1–100 Mpc

STRONGEST DOME DEFENSE:
"The aetheric medium creates an effective metric that reproduces the observed distance-redshift relation."

OUR COUNTER:
- No such metric equation published by dome model
- Flat disc geometry is NOT a valid 4D spacetime metric (no geodesics, wrong curvature)
- Even if metric could be fitted, it would differ from FLRW at high-z due to different spatial geometry
- Dome claims "zero fitted parameters" — fitting an effective metric would contradict this
