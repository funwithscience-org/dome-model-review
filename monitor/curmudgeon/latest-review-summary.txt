NEW HOLES (both minor — neither breaks the argument)
-----------------------------------------------------
  MINOR HOLE-001 (quantitative precision nit): The text reads "the
    t-squared coefficient of the longitudinal expression (~1
    arcsec/century^2), which converts to roughly 10^-24 rad/s^2."
    Technically the angular acceleration is 2x the polynomial t-squared
    coefficient (since psi = omega*t + (alpha/2)*t^2). The bare 1
    arcsec/century^2 converts to 5e-25; the actual angular acceleration
    converts to 1e-24. Both cluster at "roughly 10^-24" so the argument
    is unaffected, but a physicist nitpicking the parenthetical could
    note the factor of 2 was elided.
    Recommended fix: reword to "the second derivative of the
    longitudinal expansion (twice the t-squared coefficient of
    ~1 arcsec/cy^2)."

  MINOR HOLE-002 (citation gap): The new IAU 2006 P03 reference has
    no inline citation. The single hyperlink in the paragraph
    substantiates the velocity (50.2881 arcsec/yr) but not the
    t-squared coefficient. Recommended fix: add Capitaine, Wallace,
    Chapront (2003) A&A 412, 567 (DOI: 10.1051/0004-6361:20031539) as
    an inline link next to "IAU 2006 P_03 model."
