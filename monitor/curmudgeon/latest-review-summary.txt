MAJOR-1 [EXP450-HOLE-03, patch P2]: The 2.14 ring-magnet subsection's third paragraph
  contains an EM-incorrect sub-claim ("1/r-type decay"). Magnetic dipoles fall off as
  1/r³, not 1/r. A physicist-defender will use this single error to discredit the
  broader 1,660:1 argument even though the flux-conservation core (Σ B·A = 0 →
  B_south/B_pole = A_pole/A_south) is mathematically airtight. Fix: drop the decay-form
  paragraph entirely; the magnitude self-contradiction stands on its own.
